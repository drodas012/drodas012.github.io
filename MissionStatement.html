Our Mission Statement

Our goal is to provide support and teach skills needed to be a web developer.

Our meetings will cover a variety of coding languages needed to be successful such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

We will provide a variety of activities to keep club members involved and interested in our objectives.

The Gar-Field Web Development Club is available to all students regardless of prior knowledge or skills.

We are willing to go the extra mile and individually guide and provide support to club members that need our assistance
